About Me

Now I am a postdoc at Department of Astronomy in Nanjing University. In 2019-2020, I worked with Prof. Fumi Egusa in Institute of Astronomy of Univ. of Tokyo, as a International research student. In previous five years, I was supervised by Prof. Guilin Liu and Prof. Xu Kong at the Department of Astronomy of University of Science and Technology of China , as a PhD student.

Specialised in physical properties (gas, stellar mass, metallicity, SFR and so on) of galaxies. In the past years, I was working on the chemical processes and its evolution in star-forming galaxies in the nearby and high-redshift Universe with the data from SDSS , LAMOST , and the IFU MaNGA surveys. Currently working on the molecular properties in outflows and star-forming regions at nearby spiral galaxies with ALMA and VLT observations.

Have experience on the observation with Magellan, Hale (P200), Lijiang-2.4m, Subaru telescopes.
